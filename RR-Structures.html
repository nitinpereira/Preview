Repair/Rehabilitation of Structures

A constructed structure has generally got a service life dependent on the quality, workmanship of the construction and various other factors including materials, components, etc. They start to exhibit early deterioration harsh service environment to which a structure is exposed due to change in surrounding environment, and physical damage due to fire, explosion or earthquake.

Any repair/rehabilitation work can be carried out successfully if the reasons for distresses are known and well established.

We at F2i identify the root cause reasons for distresses of the affected structure and propose the apt solution to enhance the life of the structure. Our experts have carried out various rehabilitation work for industrial structure, water retaining structures, highway structures, marine structures, building structures
