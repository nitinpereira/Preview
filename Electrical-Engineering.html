Substation capabilities

The Electrical team has the experience and capability to provide all aspects of electrical design for substations (Both GIS and AIS from 11kV to 400kV) We can undertake large electrical substation installations for resource projects in areas such as power generation, transmission and distribution, oil and gas, mining, heavy industrial, process plants.

Power system study and relay system capability

Our engineers are well conversant with ETAP and AMTECH software and have dealt with projects having complete gamut of protection from simple electromagnetic overcurrent relay to a complex numerical generator protection system. We have experience in doing the complete configuration of numerical protection as per approved schemes.
