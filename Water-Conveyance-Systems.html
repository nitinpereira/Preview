Hydrological & Hydraulic (H&H) Modelling for Storm Water

Unplanned growth of cities across the world has resulted in inundation of water at many places. This may be due to the absence of drainage system, improper management of existing ones, existing congested drains, encroachment of drainage area for the construction of roads, filling up of water body area.

Hence it's important to include the storm water management during the initial planning stage of upcoming town/cities/commercial hubs. We have the capability to carryout H&H modelling to do the corrective measure for the existing cites.
